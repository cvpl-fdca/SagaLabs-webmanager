Instructions

This sagalabs event will be more purple teaming than you might have experienced before. We are going to split into teams of 4. Each team consists of 2 blue teamers and 2 red teamers.

The idea is to follow the attack paths and monitor incoming alarms and alerts as attacks are executed. This will help give each team a better understanding of tools, techniques, procedures, evasion tactics, etc.

During the event you will have access to different AV/EDR solutions so you can test your abilities against. The primary AV/EDR is Elastic. Using different file types and evasion techniques you will discover that some EDR solutions has blind spots.

Feel free to utilize any tool you see fit or modify the tools described in the document. This time we are going to use HardHatC2 1 as our command and control, however, if you would like to use Sliver, Mythic, Havoc, or something else feel free to do that as you will have root access to the C2 server.

You may experience the default configuration of HardHatC2 to be caught by AV/EDR while Sliver or Mythic is not as fingerprinted.
