VPN-Connection

To connect to SagaLabs through VPN, you will need to have the OpenVPN client installed on your device.
You can download the client from the OpenVPN or through your operating system's package manager.

To connect to SagaLabs using OpenVPN, follow these steps:
  • Open the OpenVPN client on your device
  • Select the configuration file provided by SagaLabs
  • Click "Connect"

Once connected, all of your internet traffic will be routed through the VPN connection, providing you with a secure and private connection to SagaLabs.
